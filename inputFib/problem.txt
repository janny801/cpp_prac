Write a program that takes 3 natural numbers from the input file 'input.txt'

and outputs every number from the Fibonacci sequence that is less than or equal to N each in a new line.

The Fibonacci sequence is the series of numbers as following: 

1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, ...


The next number is found by adding up the two numbers before it.

Fn = Fn-1 + Fn-2

Input: 
4 9 6

Output:
1 1 2 3
1 1 2 3 5 8
1 1 2 3 5
